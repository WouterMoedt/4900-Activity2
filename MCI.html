About Management Center Innsbruck


MCI Management Center Innsbruck is a privately organized business school in Innsbruck, Austria.
The university offers study programs leading to Bachelor and Master degrees as well as Executive Master programs (MBA, MSc, LL.M.), Executive Certificate programs,
Management seminars, Customized programs and research.
